Legiones ibi ueterana sexta secum reducta ceteras reliquit quo firmius esset eorum regum imperium qui neque amorem suorum habere poterant , quod fideliter permanserant in Caesaris amicitia , neque uetustatis auctoritatem , paucis diebus reges constituti .
As for the legions , taking the veteran Sixth taken away with him he left the rest there , so that the dominion of these rulers would be more stable , rulers who could enjoy neither the affection of their people , because they had remained faithfully in the friendship of Caesar , nor the authority of a long-established reign , having been in place as rulers for a small number days .
