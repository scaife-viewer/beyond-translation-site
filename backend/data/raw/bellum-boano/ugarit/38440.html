Itaque eodem tempore equites Germani dispersi uada fluminum quaerentes partim demissioribus ripis flumen tranarunt , et legionarii magnis arboribus excisis quae longitudine utramque ripam contingerent , proiectis repentinoque aggere iniecto flumen transierunt .
Thus , at the same moment a portion of the German cavalry , after scattering in search of fords , swam across the river where the banks were rather low , and the legions , after cutting down huge trees that were long enough to touch both banks , toppling them forward and piling on a makeshift roadbed , crossed the river .
