Itaque hoc animo est decertatum ut neque maritimis nauticisque sollertia atque ars praesidium ferret neque numero nauium praestantibus multitudo prodesset neque electi ad uirtutem e tanta multitudine uiri uirtuti nostrorum possent adaequare .
And so they fought in such a frame of mind that skill and cunning brought no help to the Alexandrians , though they were sailors and seafarers , and the large number did not benefit those with a greater number of ships , and men chosen for their virtue from so great a multitude of men could not equal the virtue of our men .
