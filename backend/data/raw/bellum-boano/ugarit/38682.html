Namque ut in ciuilibus dissensionibus accidere consueuit ita temporibus illis in Hispania nonnullae ciuitates rebus Cassi studebant , plures Marcello fauebant .
For as is customary to happen in civil conflicts , at that time in Spain several communities were supporting the affairs of Cassius , more were favorable toward Marcellus .
