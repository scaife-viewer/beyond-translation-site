Indicio Minuci cognoscit L. Racilium et L. Laterensem et Annium Scapulam , maximae dignitatis et gratiae prouincialem hominem sibique tam familiarem quam Laterensem et Racilium , in eadem fuisse coniuratione , nec diu moratur dolorem suum quin eos interfici iubeat .
From Minucius ' evidence he learned that Lucius Racilius , Lucius Laterensis , and Annius Scapula , a provincial of the highest standing and influence and as closely connected to himself as Laterensis and Racilius , had taken part in the same conspiracy , and he did not detain his sorrow for long before he ordered them to be killed .
