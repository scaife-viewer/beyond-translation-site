Posteroque ibi die dum suas captiuasque naues reficeret consumpto , post diem tertium contendit in insulam Issam quod eo se recepisse ex fuga credebat Octauium .
And after spending the following day there while repairing his own and the captured ships , two days later he hastened to the island of Issa , because he believed that Octavius had withdrawn himself there in flight .
