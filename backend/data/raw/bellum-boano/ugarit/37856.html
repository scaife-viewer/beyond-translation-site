Interim dissensione orta inter Achillan , qui ueterano exercitui praeerat , et Arsinoen , regis Ptolomaei minorem filiam , ut supra demonstratum est , cum uterque utrique insidiaretur et summam imperi ipse obtinere uellet , praeoccupat Arsinoe per Ganymeden eunuchum , nutricium suum , atque Achillan interficit .
Meanwhile a dispute had arisen between Achillas , who was in charge of the veteran army , and Arsinoe , the younger daughter of king Ptolemy , as was indicated above . Although each was plotting against the other and wanting to possess overall control , Arsinoe struck first through the eunuch Ganymede , her tutor , and killed Achillas .
