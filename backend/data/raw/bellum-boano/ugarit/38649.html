Quos cognoscit non sua sed Caesaris absentis causa sibi fidissimos esse nullumque periculum deprecaturos dum per eos Caesari prouincia restitueretur .
He discovered that these were extremely loyal to him not for his own sake but for the absent Caesar , and that they would refuse no danger provided that the province was restored to Caesar through their agency .
