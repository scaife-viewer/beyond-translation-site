Nemo enim aut in prouincia natus , ut uernaculae legionis milites , aut diuturnitate iam factus prouincialis , quo in numero erat secunda legio , non cum omni prouincia consenserat in odio Cassi .
For nobody either born in the province , like the soldiers of the indigenous legion , or effectively provincial from their long stay ( in this number was the second legion ) , did not agree with the entire province in their hatred for Cassius .
