Progressus secunda ut hiberna tempestate cum in Hiberum flumen noctis uitandae causa se contulisset , inde paulo uehementiore tempestate — nihilo periculosius se nauigaturum credens — profectus , aduersis fluctibus occurrentibus ostio fluminis , in ipsis faucibus , cum neque flectere nauem propter uim fluminis neque derectam tantis fluctibus tenere posset , demersa naue periit .
When he , having made progress in weather that was favorable for winter , had put into the Ebro river to avoid the night , setting out thence , despite the storm growing a little more violent , believing that he would sail no more dangerously than before , with the incoming waves blocking the river mouth he perished right in the narrow outlet after his ship sank since he could neither maneuver his ship because of the river ’ s force nor keep it straight in such great waves .
