Ille , ut ex carceribus in liberum cursum emissus , adeo contra Caesarem acriter bellum gerere coepit ut lacrimas quas in colloquio proiecerat gaudio uideretur profudisse .
As if released from from the starting gate onto an open track , Ptolemy began to wage so vigorous a war against Caesar that he seemed to have shed from joy the tears that he had produced in their meeting .
