Quod ubi conspexit celeriter uela subduci demittique antemnas iubet et milites armari et uexillo sublato , quo pugnandi dabat signum , quae primae naues subsequebantur idem ut facerent significabat .
When he noticed this , he quickly ordered that the sails be reefed , the yards be lowered , and that the soldiers arm themselves . Having raised the banner with which he was accustomed to give the battle-sign , he started signaling that the ships that were right behind him do the same thing .
