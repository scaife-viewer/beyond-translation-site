Quo ut uentum est , paucis defendentibus munitionem , nostris contra militibus acerrime pugnantibus , diuerso clamore et proelio perterriti Alexandrini trepidantes in omnes partes castrorum discurrere coeperunt .
Upon their arrival , with a small number defending the fortifications while our men fought with extreme vigor , the Alexandrians , terrified by the noise and the fighting in a new location , began to scatter in a panic into every part of the stronghold .
