Diuersis enim telis nostri figebantur , aduersi ex uallo castrorum , auersi ex flumine , in quo multae naues instructae funditoribus et sagittariis nostros impugnabant .
For our men were being pierced by projectiles from different directions , from the front by projectiles coming from the stronghold rampart , from the rear by projectiles coming from the river , on which many ships manned with slingers and archers were attacking our men .
