Cum duae legiones reliquae essent in castris secundani , ueriti ne soli relinquerentur atque ex eo quid sensissent indicaretur , secuti sunt factum superiorum .
When two legions were left in the camp , the soldiers of the second legion , fearing lest they be left solitary and lest it become evident from that what their feelings were , followed the lead of the previous ones .
